Our Services

We are trying to fix the problem of scooters parking in NCKU. Because of lots of students parking their scooter without rule and management, making others a big problem. we want create a new platform for any devices to search, to publish, to report anything the user encountered.


Search

For many people, parking scooters is a daily usual. However, there is no enough space to hold total of scooters. And this makes lots of problems. We design a platform to let people search how many remaining space in parking lot really time and convenient.

Report

As over-hold people want to park scooter in parking lot, most of them would park illegally in entrys or alleys. We need a report system to manage this.

Publish

For someone parking illegally on sidewalks, we provide a feature to let someone publish the drawing information, like where and when.
